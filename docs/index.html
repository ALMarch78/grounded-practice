📚 About These Worksheets

These comprehensive worksheets are designed to accompany The Grounded Practice app. They provide structured guidance through each module, helping you:

Deep exploration of your patterns, thoughts, and emotions

Work through exercises to build new coping strategies

Document your journey and celebrate your growth

Revisit worksheets at any time as you continue your practice
