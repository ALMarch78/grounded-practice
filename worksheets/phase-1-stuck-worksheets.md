## Module 2: Know Your Nervous System
### Window of Tolerance & Threat Detection

**Duration:** 15-20 min

---

### Worksheet 2.1: Your Window of Tolerance

Your nervous system has a "window" where you feel safe and able to handle life. Outside this window, you're either hyperaroused (panicked) or hypoaroused (numb).

**Hyperaroused signs (I'm too amped up):**
- [ ] Racing heart
- [ ] Restlessness, fidgeting
- [ ] Worry/racing thoughts
- [ ] Irritability
- [ ] Sleep problems
- [ ] Other: _______________

**Resourced state (I'm in my window):**
- [ ] Calm and focused
- [ ] Able to think clearly
- [ ] Feeling connected to others
- [ ] Able to handle challenges
- [ ] Sleeping okay
- [ ] Other: _______________

**Hypoaroused signs (I'm checked out):**
- [ ] Numbness, disconnection
- [ ] Heaviness, fatigue
- [ ] Blank mind, can't focus
- [ ] Difficulty speaking or moving
- [ ] Despair, hopelessness
- [ ] Other: _______________
